## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Create your first study plan by clicking "Create New Study Plan"
3. Use the timer to track your study sessions
4. Monitor your progress in the dashboard

## How to Use

### Creating Study Plans
1. Click "Create New Study Plan"
2. Enter subject name, study duration, and difficulty level
3. Click "Create Plan" to save

### Using the Timer
1. Set your desired study duration (default: 25 minutes)
2. Click "Start" to begin your study session
3. The timer will count down and alert you when finished
4. Study time is automatically added to your total hours

### Viewing Analytics
- Check the Analytics section for insights into your study patterns
- View daily study time charts
- See completion rates and productivity statistics

### Data Backup
- Click "Export Data" to download your study data
- Use "Import Data" to restore from a previous backup
